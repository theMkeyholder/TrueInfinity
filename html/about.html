True Infinity

How to Play

Antimatter is the primary currency in True Infinity, as True Infinity is a knockoff of Infinite Layers (but with more content) which is a knockoff of Antimatter Dimensions. Antimatter is used to buy stuff. The objective of this game is to get so much antimatter that the games big number library can't work with it anymore. At this point you can say that you beat the game. At the moment that would take you approximately {10, {10, 16}, 1000} (BAN) years to do so good luck!!
Dimensions are what produce your resources in True Infinity. The First Dimension produces antimatter, the Second Dimension produces First Dimensions, the Third produces Seconds and so on. After 10 dimensions, the game only stores three dimensions, this is to let the game handle 1e308th dimensions. I then used a mathematical approximation using binomial coefficients to approximate the production of first dimensions. After you get to e1e6 antimatter, all dimensions get a huge boost to their multipliers.
Max All is your best friend. It allows you to buy max many dimensions at once, and unlock more dimensions far more quickly, letting you buy several thousands, or billions of dimensions at a time. You can automate the Max All later in the game.
Once you reach 1.79e308 antimatter you will be able to infinity. This will give you a certain number of infinity points, set your antimatter to 10 and delete all your dimension, but you'll be able to get way further in the future :) Once you reach 1.79e308 infinity points you can go to eternity, once you get 1.79e308 eternity points you can reality, etc.
Upon prestiging you will get some points for that prestige layer. You can use these points to buy that layers dimensions. Same thing as for regular dimensions. First makes power, Second makes First, etc.
A layers dimensions make that layers power. Power provides a multiplier boost to all layers below it. For the first layer below it, the boost is equal to the amount of power. For the second layer and further layers down, the boost is huge in comparison, like double exponentiation huge!
Once you Celerity (7th layer of prestige) you unlock the automation tab. You can then spend that layers points on automating the max-all button and getting automatic pretige points based on the points of that layer. Each game tick (20 times a second) it gives you 1e6th the amount of points you would've got if you'd prestiged at that point. The prestige automation is far more expensive than the max all automation. Prestige automation does nothing on the highest layer you have.
Once you reach eee100 antimatter, you can automate the process of auto-max all and auto-prestige. This lets you click your way to high prestige layers, and automatically be able to max all for the higer layers.
Once you reach P200 (the 200th prestige layer) you can unlock Auto-Prestige (seperate from Auto-Prestige Gain mentioned above) for eee500 antimatter. Auto-Prestige unlocks new layers for you.
You can reach the next level of auto prestige for eee3000 antimatter, at this level the auto prestige unlocks 10 layers at a time. The next level costs eee8000 and unlocks 100 layers at a time. Level 4 costs eee50000 and unlocks layers at 1.1 times the layer number of your highest layer. Level 5 is very powerful and costs eeee10 antimatter, but unlocks layers at your highest layer to the power of 1.1;
